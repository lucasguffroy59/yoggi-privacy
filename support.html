Frequently Asked Questions

  • The voice feature isn't working. Make sure you have granted microphone permission to the app in your device settings.
  • My conversation history disappeared. History is linked to your device. Reinstalling the app will reset it.
  • I want to delete my data. Send us an email and we will erase all data associated with your account within 30 days.
  • The AI isn't responding. Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, contact us.
